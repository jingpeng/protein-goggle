Example Data

The following raw data from Thermo Orbitrap instruments as well as their search results using ProteinGoggle are open to anybody who wants to have a feeling about what and how ProteinGoggle is doing. You are also welcome to take the raw data to run on other accessible top-down intact protein database search engines, compare the results side-by-side, have a direct feeling of strength and weakness of different search engines, and finally choose specific one(s) to satisfy your specific scientific need(s).
