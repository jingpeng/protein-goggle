Welcome

Welcome to ProteinGoggle, an intact protein database search engine using our novel isotopic mass-to-charge ratio & envelope fingerprinting (iMEF) search algorithm! iMEF intrinsically has the ability to interpret tandem mass spectral data in situ (without deconvolution or deisotoping) and thus has its unique strength of unwrap overlapping isotopic envelopes (IEs) accurately and efficiently and distinctly separate matching fragments with ideal IEs from non-matching fragments with non-ideal IEs. These strength together with strict quality (tolerance) control of m/z and relative abundance down to every isotopic peak in every IE (both precursor & fragments), ProteinGoggle delivers confident protein identification.

With about three years’ development and lots of people’s input and contribution, ProteinGoggle now is ready for top-down qualitative intact protein identification from collision-based, electron-based or photon-based tandem mass spectra of individual proteins using direct infusion or proteome mixture using LC separation.

So, Please give it try by looking at the “Example Data” or “Submit a Job”!

Our goal is “to interpret every tandem mass spectrum accurately and efficiently and deliver the results in the way you want”! Should you have any comments, suggestions, or specific needs, please feel free to contact us!
